## 👤 About Me

> 🎓 **1st year B.Tech student** in Computer Science with an AI/ML specialization  
> 📱 I build **iOS apps with Swift** — from mood trackers to productivity tools, I ship things that actually work  
> 🤖 Passionate about the intersection of **AI and mobile** — training models that live in your pocket  
> 🛡️ Exploring **AI security** through red-teaming and adversarial ML — because building smart systems means knowing how they break  
> 🚀 Currently: **learning fast, shipping faster**
